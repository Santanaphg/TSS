About Us

In today's interconnected world, the efficiency and reliability of logistics services are crucial for bussinesses operating on a global scale.
The Shipping Store (TSS) was founded to provide our customers with the tools and support they need to ship their packages efficiently at the best possible rates.


With over 25 years of industry experience, our partners have created TSS @The.ShippingStore to offer a one-stop shop for all your shipping needs. Enjoy the convenience of shipping nationally and internationally with our partners, DHL®, UPS®, and FedEx®, from the comfort of your home or office. You'll have the option to import products from other countries as well.

Want to use our Service?

It's easy!
Just complete our quote form with the following data:

  • 1. Where is your package coming from?
  • 2. Where is the package going?
  • 3. What does the package contain?
  • 4. How much is it worth for insurance and for custom purpose?
  • 5. Dimensions and weight.
  • 6. Just pick your favorite courier: DHL® , FedEx®, or UPS®. Or, we can give you prices for all of them.
  • 7. Choose Pick-up or drop-off.
  • 8. With this information, we will send you a quote with different prices options for you to choose.
  • 9. Once the quote is approved, we will send you the payment link.
  • 10. Once the payment has been made, we will send you the shipping label to place it on the package. Then, according to your preference, we can proceed to schedule a pick-up or you can drop-off the package in the nearest courier station.
